About Us

Welcome to the West Visayas State University Cybersecurity Department, where we pioneer cutting-edge digital security education and research. Our department is committed to developing the next generation of cybersecurity professionals.

Through innovative programs, hands-on training, and industry partnerships, we prepare students to tackle the evolving challenges in cybersecurity and information assurance.
